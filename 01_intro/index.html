I will design and fabricate panels using the laser cutter or 3D printer, with alternating hydrophilic and hydrophobic surfaces to help droplets form and flow. The panels will be mounted on a base that can tilt based on input from sensors measuring humidity, temperature, and wind. If time allows, I may explore adding haptic feedback to alert users to high-pressure areas. My goal is to create a functional and informative insole that helps users improve their foot health and comfort.
